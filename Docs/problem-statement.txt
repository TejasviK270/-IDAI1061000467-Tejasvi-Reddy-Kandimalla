PROBLEM STATEMENT

"How might we design a smart, personalized, and instantly accessible stress-relief experience for employees that fits within a short work break, reduces mental and physical stress, and seamlessly integrates into their daily workflow — without disrupting their productivity?"

Supporting Context:
Employees across different roles, ages, and stress levels lack access to immediate, private, and personalized relaxation environments at the workplace. Existing wellness solutions are either too generic, too time-consuming, or unavailable when employees need them most. The Smart Stress Relief Pod, managed through a mobile app, aims to bridge this gap by combining biometric data, AI personalization, and sensory therapy into a single, easy-to-use solution.
