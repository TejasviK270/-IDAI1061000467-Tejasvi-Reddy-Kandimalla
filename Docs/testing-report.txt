TASKS GIVEN TO USERS:
—————————————————————————
Task 1: Log in and find your current stress level on the dashboard.
Task 2: Complete the MoodSync AI stress check-in and start a pod session.
Task 3: Navigate to the Breathe With Me feature and complete a 3-minute session.
Task 4: Book a pod session for the next available afternoon slot.
Task 5: Complete the post-session journal and rate your session.
Task 6: Find your stress trend chart and identify your best day this week.

—————————————————————————
OBSERVATIONS:
—————————————————————————
- 7/8 users successfully completed all 6 tasks without assistance.
- 5/8 users said the MoodSync AI check-in was "easy and quick."
- 3/8 users initially missed the Pod Scheduler — the navigation icon was unclear.
- 2/8 users (both 50+) found the text on the Pod Control sliders too small.
- All 8 users rated the Breathe With Me animation as "calming" and "intuitive."
- 6/8 users said the Post-Session Journal made them feel "motivated to return."

—————————————————————————
KEY IMPROVEMENTS MADE AFTER TESTING:
—————————————————————————
1. ISSUE: Pod Scheduler icon was unclear (3 users confused).
   CHANGE: Replaced abstract icon with a calendar icon and added a "Schedule" text label below it.

2. ISSUE: Text on Pod Control sliders was too small for older users (2 users).
   CHANGE: Increased slider label font from 12px to 15px. Added larger aroma/lighting icons.

3. ISSUE: Users wanted to know what each aroma smells like before selecting it.
   CHANGE: Added a tooltip/info icon next to each aroma option with a 1-line description (e.g., "Lavender — calming, floral, reduces anxiety").

4. ISSUE: 2 users felt the stress scale (1–10) was confusing.
   CHANGE: Replaced the number scale with emoji + color coding (green = calm, red = very stressed) for instant comprehension.

5. ISSUE: The dashboard felt data-heavy on first visit for low-tech users.
   CHANGE: Added a "Welcome Tour" overlay for first-time users explaining each section in 3 simple steps.

—————————————————————————
CONCLUSION:
—————————————————————————
Testing confirmed that the ZenPod app prototype is largely intuitive, visually calming, and well-received across age groups. The iterations made after the first round of testing significantly improved accessibility, navigation clarity, and emotional engagement. A second round of testing (post-iteration) showed an average satisfaction increase from 4.2 to 4.6 out of 5.
