IDEATION DOCUMENT — MINDFUL WORK APP

BRAINSTORMING METHOD: Mind mapping + "How Might We" questions + dot voting

CORE PROBLEM: Employees need instant, personalized, private stress relief that fits within a 5–15 minute work break.

—————————————————————————
5 COMPULSORY FEATURES (Required by assessment):
—————————————————————————

1. MOODSYNC AI
Description: The app's core intelligence engine. Collects stress input from the user via a quick survey (1–5 stress level), wearable data (heart rate, HRV), or voice tone analysis. Based on this data, MoodSync AI automatically configures the pod settings — selecting the most suitable music playlist, lighting color, aroma intensity, and meditation type.
Why it's needed: Personalization is the #1 employee request. Generic settings don't work. MoodSync removes the need for the user to manually configure anything, delivering instant relief.
How it works in the app: A stress check-in screen on launch → AI processes the input → Auto-configures pod → Confirmation screen shows the session plan.

2. BREATHE WITH ME
Description: A guided micro-meditation feature offering 3 session lengths: 3 minutes, 7 minutes, and 12 minutes. Uses animated breathing circles, calming visuals, and audio narration to guide the user through inhale-hold-exhale cycles. Includes optional body scan and visualization techniques.
Why it's needed: Breathing is the fastest evidence-based method for reducing cortisol (stress hormone). This makes it accessible even outside the pod.
How it works in the app: Available from the Home screen and Pod Control Screen. A circular breathing animation expands/contracts with audio cues.

3. POD SCHEDULER
Description: A calendar-based booking system where employees reserve a pod in advance. Shows real-time availability, allows employees to pick their preferred pod type (quiet reflection, active reset, aroma therapy), and sends a reminder notification 5 minutes before the session.
Why it's needed: Eliminates crowding and anxiety about pod availability. Employees who can plan their break feel more in control and actually use wellness resources.
How it works in the app: Calendar view showing available slots → Select type → Confirm booking → Get reminder notification.

4. POST-SESSION JOURNAL
Description: After each pod session, the app prompts the user with 3 simple questions: "How stressed were you before? (1–10)", "How do you feel now? (1–10)", and "What helped most today?" Users can also write free-text notes. Data is saved to the Stress Tracking Dashboard.
Why it's needed: Reflection reinforces the benefit of the session and builds long-term stress awareness. Journaling also helps users identify their most effective stress triggers and solutions.
How it works in the app: Appears automatically after each session ends. Optional — user can skip.

5. STRESS TRACKING DASHBOARD
Description: A visual data dashboard on the Home Screen that displays the user's stress trends over time. Includes a weekly stress chart (line graph), total pod sessions completed, average stress reduction per session, and a mood streak tracker. Users can also view AI-generated insights like "You're 30% calmer on days when you book a morning session."
Why it's needed: Long-term data helps users understand their stress patterns and empowers them to make healthier choices. It also demonstrates the value of the pod to the employer.
How it works in the app: Displayed prominently on the Home/Dashboard screen. Tap any data point to see session details.

—————————————————————————
2 ADDITIONAL INNOVATIVE FEATURES:
—————————————————————————

6. WELLNESS REWARD BADGES
Description: A gamification layer that awards digital badges for consistent pod usage (e.g., "7-Day Streak", "Stress Buster", "Zen Master"). Badges appear on the user's profile and can be shared with an opt-in workplace wellness leaderboard.
Why it's needed: Gamification increases long-term engagement and motivates employees to use wellness resources consistently rather than only during crisis moments.

7. EMERGENCY CALM BUTTON
Description: A one-tap "I need help now" button accessible from any screen. Instantly triggers a 3-minute guided breathing session, dims the pod lights to blue, and activates the lightest aroma setting — no configuration required.
Why it's needed: During sudden stress spikes (panic before a meeting, overwhelming news), users can't navigate menus. This feature provides instant relief with zero friction.
