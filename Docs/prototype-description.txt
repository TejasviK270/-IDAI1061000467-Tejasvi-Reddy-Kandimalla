PROTOTYPE DESCRIPTION — MINDFUL WORK APP
Tool Used: Figma 

SCREEN 1: LOGIN / HOME DASHBOARD
Purpose: Entry point and main hub of the app.
Contents:
- App logo: "ZenPod" with a lotus icon
- Greeting: "Good afternoon, [User Name]. Your stress level today: 7/10"
- Stress Tracking Dashboard widget (mini line chart showing this week's stress levels)
- Quick-action buttons: "Book a Pod", "Start Breathe With Me", "View Progress"
- Bottom navigation bar: Home | Schedule | Journal | Profile
Color Scheme: Soft teal (#E1F5EE) background, white cards, dark teal text
Design Notes: Clean, calming layout. No harsh colors. Large readable fonts.

SCREEN 2: STRESS CHECK-IN (MOODSYNC AI)
Purpose: Collects stress data to configure the pod automatically.
Contents:
- Title: "How are you feeling right now?"
- Emoji stress scale (1–5): 😊 → 😐 → 😟 → 😤 → 😰
- Optional: "Connect wearable" toggle (shows heart rate: 92 BPM)
- Optional: "Analyse my voice" mic button
- CTA Button: "Configure My Pod" (teal)
- Short text: "MoodSync AI will personalise your session."
Design Notes: Friendly, non-clinical. Emojis make it approachable for all ages.

SCREEN 3: POD CONTROL SCREEN
Purpose: Allows manual or AI-driven adjustment of pod settings.
Contents:
- Header: "Your Pod is Ready — Session: Calming Reset"
- Three sliders: Lighting (Blue → Warm White), Aroma (Lavender → Citrus), Sound (Rain → Ocean → Silence)
- AI Recommendation badge: "MoodSync suggests: Blue light + Lavender + Ocean sounds"
- Timer: "Session: 12 minutes" (adjustable)
- Buttons: "Start Session" | "Customise Manually"
Design Notes: Use visual color swatches for lighting options. Icons for each aroma type.

SCREEN 4: BREATHE WITH ME
Purpose: Guided micro-meditation during or outside the pod.
Contents:
- Animated breathing circle (expands on inhale, contracts on exhale)
- Text prompts: "Breathe in... 1... 2... 3... 4" / "Hold... 1... 2... 3... 4" / "Breathe out..."
- Session length selector: 3 min | 7 min | 12 min
- Background: Deep blue gradient (simulate with flat dark blue in prototype)
- Audio icon (indicates narration playing)
Design Notes: Minimal — nothing on screen but the breathing circle and prompts. Calming.

SCREEN 5: POD SCHEDULER
Purpose: Book a pod session in advance.
Contents:
- Monthly calendar view with available (green dot) and booked (grey) slots
- Pod type selector: "Quiet Reflection" | "Aroma Therapy" | "Active Reset"
- Time slot grid: 09:00 | 10:30 | 13:00 | 15:30 | 17:00
- Confirmation card: "Pod 3 booked for Wednesday 2:00 PM — Aroma Therapy"
- Toggle: "Remind me 5 minutes before" (on by default)
Design Notes: Simple calendar. Color-coded availability. Confirmation screen with summary.

SCREEN 6: POST-SESSION JOURNAL
Purpose: Reflective journaling after each session.
Contents:
- Header: "Session Complete! How do you feel?"
- Before stress rating: 1–10 slider (pre-filled from check-in)
- After stress rating: 1–10 slider (user fills in)
- Question: "What helped most today?" (checkboxes: Music / Breathing / Lighting / Aroma / All)
- Free text box: "Add a note (optional)"
- Button: "Save Journal Entry"
- Motivational message: "You reduced your stress by 3 points today. Great work!"
Design Notes: Warm, encouraging tone. Positive colors (soft green/teal).

SCREEN 7: STRESS TRACKING DASHBOARD
Purpose: Long-term view of stress trends and progress.
Contents:
- Weekly line chart: Stress level Mon–Sun (Y-axis 1–10)
- Stat cards: "Sessions this week: 4" | "Avg. stress reduction: 3.2 pts" | "Current streak: 5 days"
- AI Insight: "You're 28% calmer on mornings when you use the pod before 10am."
- Badge display: "Zen Streak 🏅" | "Stress Buster 🏅"
- Button: "View Full History"
Design Notes: Data should feel empowering, not clinical. Use soft greens and teals for positive data.
