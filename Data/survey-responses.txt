SURVEY RESPONSES — MINDFUL WORK APP RESEARCH

Survey: Workplace Stress & Relaxation Preferences
Participants: 10 anonymous employees
Method: Online Google Form

Q1: How often do you feel stressed at work?
- Daily: 8/10
- A few times a week: 2/10
- Rarely: 0/10

Q2: What are your top 3 stress triggers? (select all that apply)
- Tight deadlines: 9/10
- Back-to-back meetings: 7/10
- Difficult team communication: 6/10
- Unclear instructions: 5/10
- Noisy environment: 4/10

Q3: Do you currently have a private, calm space at work to decompress?
- No: 7/10
- Yes, but rarely use it: 2/10
- Yes, regularly: 1/10

Q4: How long could you spare for a stress-relief break during work hours?
- Less than 5 minutes: 2/10
- 5–10 minutes: 5/10
- 10–15 minutes: 3/10

Q5: Which relaxation methods do you prefer?
- Music: 8/10
- Deep breathing: 6/10
- Short walk: 5/10
- Aromatherapy: 3/10
- Meditation: 4/10
- Nothing — I push through: 3/10

Q6: Would you use a bookable wellness pod at your workplace?
- Definitely yes: 6/10
- Probably yes: 3/10
- Unsure: 1/10

Q7: How comfortable are you using apps or wearables for wellness?
- Very comfortable: 4/10
- Somewhat comfortable: 4/10
- Not comfortable: 2/10

Q8: What is most important to you in a stress-relief tool?
- Privacy: 8/10
- Personalization: 7/10
- Speed (works fast): 6/10
- Ease of use: 7/10
- Being able to plan/schedule: 5/10

KEY INSIGHT: 80% of employees want a private, personalized, quick stress-relief solution. 90% experience daily or near-daily stress. Booking in advance was rated important by 50%.
